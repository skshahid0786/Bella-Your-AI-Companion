2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to provide the Bella experience:

  • Account data — your name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Auth)
  • Profile data — your display name, birthday (optional), and avatar preference
  • Usage data — message count, login streak, hearts balance, and premium status
  • Chat history — stored locally on your device only (localStorage), never on our servers
  • Gallery images — stored locally on your device only
  • Settings — theme, sound, and notification preferences (stored locally)
💜 Your chat messages are never stored on our servers. They live only on your device and are deleted when you clear your chat.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Create and manage your Bella account
  • Track your login streak and reward bonus hearts
  • Remember your preferences and settings across sessions
  • Show birthday surprises if you provide your birthdate
  • Manage your hearts balance and premium status

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of the services listed below.
